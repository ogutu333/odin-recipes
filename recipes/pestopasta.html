Pesto Pasta

Description

A vibrant, herbaceous dish that captures the essence of fresh, sun-kissed ingredients. With each bite, you'll experience the silky texture of al dente pasta coated in a luscious, emerald-green pesto sauce. The pesto, made from fragrant basil, rich pine nuts, sharp Parmesan cheese, and a splash of olive oil, offers a punch of fresh, nutty, and garlicky flavors. The simplicity of the ingredients allows the natural goodness to shine, transforming pasta into a lively, aromatic meal that feels like a taste of the Mediterranean in every forkful.

Ingredients

Pesto Sauce

  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves tightly pack measuring cup
  • 1/2 cup parmesan finely grated
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 garlic clove small
  • 7 tbsp extra virgin olive oil best quality

Pasta

  • 300g-350g of pasta of your choice penne, spaghetti or linguinne etc
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 3/4 cup pasta cooking water
  • Parmesan, for serving

Instructions

Pesto Sauce:

  1. Toast Pine Nuts – Preheat small skillet over medium high heat (no oil). Add pine nuts and toast until light brown. Immediately remove and let cool slightly.
  2. Blitz Pesto - Place all ingredients except olive oil in a food processor or blender. Blitz until finely chopped.
  3. Add oil while blitzing – With the motor running, slowly pour the oil in through the feeder tube. Blitz until smooth (or to desired consistency), adding a touch of extra oil (or water) if required to help it blitz.
  4. Handheld blender stick – Place all ingredients (including oil) in a bowl. Blitz with hand held blender until smooth.

Pesto Pasta

  1. Bring a large pot of water to the boil with the salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook for the length of time per the packet.
  3. Just before draining, scoop out 1 cup of of the pasta cooking water.
  4. Drain pasta in a colander, leave it for a minute.
  5. Transfer pasta to a bowl (do not use pasta cooking pot, too hot).
  6. Add pesto and 1/4 cup of pasta water. Toss to coat pasta in pesto, adding more water if required to make pasta silky and saucy, rather than dry and sticky.
  7. Taste, add more salt and pepper if desired.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parmesan.
